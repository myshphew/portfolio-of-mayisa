The Canvas of Sabekun Nahar

Whispers of Mayisa...

With a curious heart and a creative soul, Mayisa walks the line where science meets art. A student of Biotechnology at BRAC University, she seeks to understand the hidden codes of life, while her brushes and lens capture the colors and stories that words cannot always tell.
Her paintings whisper emotions, her photographs hold fragments of fleeting moments, and her love for cats adds a gentle warmth to her days. Whether she is blending shades on canvas, framing light through her camera, or exchanging smiles with new friends, Mayisa creates connections — between people, between nature, and between dreams and reality.
To her, creativity is not just an act, but a way of being — a way of listening to the world, of shaping beauty from simplicity, and of weaving stories from silence. In the quiet embrace of nature, she often finds her truest self, rooted yet free, still yet flowing.
A dreamer, a creator, and a seeker of wonder — Mayisa continues to paint her journey in colors only she can see.
